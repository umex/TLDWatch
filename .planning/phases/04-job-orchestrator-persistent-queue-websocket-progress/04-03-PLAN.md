<objective>
Plan 04-03 delivers WebSocket progress broadcasting (SC-3) and idempotent job submission (SC-5) for Phase 04.

Purpose: Clients observe live transcription progress without polling; duplicate submissions (retry, double-click, network flap) collapse to the same job instead of spawning wasted GPU work. Grounded in D-07 (idempotent submit), D-08 (WS progress pub/sub), D-09 (per-chunk progress + ETA). The ETA computation and event enrichment are ALREADY handled by 04-01's orchestrator (it publishes progress events with `percent` and `eta_s`, hiding ETA until `chunks_done >= 2`); 04-03 RELAYS those events to WS clients and adds the state-snapshot-on-connect. 04-03 does NOT re-compute ETA or re-publish events.

Output: `app/api/routes_ws.py` (WS endpoint + subscriber cap + snapshot), `app/api/idempotency.py` (header validation + lookup/insert), `migrations/0008_idempotency_keys.sql`, `app/main.py` (bus on app.state), test files (test_ws.py, test_idempotency.py, test_event_bus.py extensions).
