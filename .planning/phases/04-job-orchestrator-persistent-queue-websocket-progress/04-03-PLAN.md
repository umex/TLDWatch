Plan 04-03 delivers WebSocket progress broadcasting (SC-3) and idempotent job submission (SC-5) for Phase 04.

Purpose: Clients observe live transcription progress without polling; duplicate submissions (retry, double-click, network flap) collapse to the same job instead of spawning wasted GPU work.
