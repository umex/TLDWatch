<objective>
Plan 04-04 closes CR-03 from 04-VERIFICATION.md: the state-machine dead-end where `run_job` cannot advance a job to `done` on resume when both `skip_ingested` and `skip_transcribed` are True but `manifest.current_stage != "done"`.

Purpose: A crash between `update_stage("transcribed")` (orchestrator.py:276-279) and `update_stage("done")` (orchestrator.py:280-281) leaves the job stuck in `transcribing` forever — `transcript.json` is on disk (file-as-truth says transcribed is complete), but the derived `done` transition was never recorded. `infer_resume_point` returns `"done"` in this case, but `run_job` only early-returns when `resume_stage is None` — it does not act on `resume_stage == "done"`. The two `if not skip_*` blocks both fall through (nothing to do), and the function exits without advancing. This breaks SC-1 (the state machine must reach `done`) and SC-2 (restart resume must recover in-flight crash windows).

Output: A 3-line fix in `app/jobs/orchestrator.py` (a final branch after the two `if not skip_*` blocks that advances to `done` when `resume_stage == "done"`) + a new regression test in `tests/test_orchestrator.py` that simulates the crash window and asserts re-entry advances the job to done.
</objective>
